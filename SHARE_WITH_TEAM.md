# 🔧 Engineered Safety Platform — Share This with Your Team

## How to Use

1. **Download** the file: `Engineered-Safety-Platform-Standalone.html`
2. **Open** it in any web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
3. **Select your role** when prompted:
   - **Project Team Member** — Submit safety ideas, browse solutions
   - **Engineered Safety Lead** — Review and approve ideas
   - **Project Leadership** — Final approvals and implementation tracking
4. **Start using!** All data is demo/sample data for testing

---

## What You Can Do

### 💡 Submit an Idea (Team Members)
- 4-step form with validation
- Describe the problem and your solution
- Select relevant Fatal & Severe Risk (FSR) categories
- Attach supporting files (photos, drawings)
- Auto-saved drafts

### 🔍 Search Solutions (Everyone)
- Browse library of approved safety innovations
- Filter by sector, status, phase, implementation stage
- View detailed solution cards with ratings, comments, timeline
- LOR Preferred / Case Study badges

### ✅ Review Ideas (ESL Role)
- Queue of submitted ideas awaiting review
- Overdue alert (items >7 days pending)
- Approve → forward to Project Leadership
- Return with feedback → submitter revises and resubmits
- Comment and collaboration features

### 📊 Leadership Dashboard (Leadership Role)
- Pending approvals queue
- KPI cards: submissions, approvals, implementation timeline
- 6-month submission trends
- Escalated items alerts
- Implementation action tracking by assignee
- Ideas breakdown by sector

## Current Roles & Workflows

### Role: Project Team Member
**Access:**
- Home Dashboard
- Submit Idea (new)
- Search Solutions Library

**Cannot:**
- Review other ideas
- See review queue
- Approve ideas

### Role: Engineered Safety Lead (ESL)
**Access:**
- Home Dashboard  
- My Reviews (your review queue)
- Search Solutions Library

**Can:**
- Review submitted ideas
- Approve ideas → forward to Leadership
- Return with feedback → submitter revises
- Comment and collaborate

**Cannot:**
- Submit ideas as team member
- Make final approval decisions

### Role: Project Leadership
**Access:**
- Home Dashboard
- Reports & Approvals
- Search Solutions Library

**Can:**
- See ESL-approved ideas awaiting final decision
- Approve for implementation
- Return with feedback
- View implementation actions & progress
- See escalation alerts & trends
